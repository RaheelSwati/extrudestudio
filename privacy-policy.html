Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Extrude Gaming Studio ("we", "our", "us"), operated by Raheel Swati / Haroon Swati, collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use our mobile applications ("Apps") available on Amazon Appstore, Google Play, and Apple App Store.

1. Introduction

By installing or using our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

A. User Provided Data

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or address.

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device model & operating system
  • Approximate country/region
  • IP address (for analytics and security)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Gameplay events
  • Advertising IDs (GAID, IDFA, Amazon Advertising ID)

C. Location Data

We may collect approximate, non-GPS location data. We do NOT collect precise or background GPS location.

3. Permissions Used

  • Internet and network access
  • Storage (save game progress)
  • Approximate location
  • Vibration
  • Wake lock (keep screen active)

4. How We Use Data

  • Improve gameplay and performance
  • Show non-personalized ads
  • Analyze crashes and bugs
  • Detect fraud or abuse

5. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may integrate with:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads
  • Amazon Ads
  • AppLovin
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Unity Analytics

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Data Retention

Data is kept only as long as required for the App to function.

8. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request access to your data
  • Opt-out of personalized ads through device settings

9. Do Not Track

Our Apps do not respond to Do-Not-Track signals.

10. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards but no method is 100% secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

Updates may be made. Continued use means acceptance of changes.
